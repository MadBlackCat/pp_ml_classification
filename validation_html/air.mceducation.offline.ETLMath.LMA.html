Privacy Policy

Welcome to ETL Learning which is owned by Educational Technologies Limited. Your privacy is important to us. To better protect your privacy, we provide this notice explaining our online information practices.

You need to provide us with the information we have requested for a Free Presentation. We will use the information provided by you in the course of our dealing with you to process your participation in the requested for Free Presentation of this web site.

We may collect anonymous information about your use of this website from cookies and by the use of pixel tags. We may match this information with any other data you have given us for the purposes of tailoring our site to help you, sending you appropriate marketing communications and maintaining our records. A cookie is a short piece of data which is sent from a web server to a web browser when that browser visits the server’s site. It records information about the different websites a user has visited. Pixel tags are used to read cookies. Most browsers automatically accept cookies. You can set your browser options so that you will not receive cookies and you can also delete existing cookies from your browser. However some of the cookies sent to your browser will help us to save you time, for example by remembering your contact details when you are visiting the site for the second time – this means that you will not have to complete your contact details section again unless they have changed. In addition you may find that some parts of the site will not function properly if you refuse cookies.

To the extent permitted under applicable local laws, we may share your information with other members of our group, including certain offices in Hong Kong and Singapore for internal management and administration purposes and other reasonable commercial purposes. We may also share your information with certain reputable third parties in order to provide the Online Testing service portion of this website.

It is possible that we could merge or be acquired by another business. If this happens, we may share the information that relates to you. You will be sent notice of such an event. Please note that clicking on to links and banner advertisements may result in your transfer to another website, where data privacy practices may be different to ours. Visitors should consult the other website’s privacy policies as we are not responsible for, and have no control over, information that is submitted to or collected by these third parties.

We will disclose information we maintain when required to do so by law, for example, in response to a court order or a subpoena. We also may disclose such information in response to a law enforcement agency’s request.

This privacy policy may be changed by us at any time. The revised policy will be posted to this page so that you are always aware of the information we collect, how we use it and under what circumstances we disclose it. We will ensure that your personal information is treated with the utmost respect and in accordance with applicable personal data privacy legislation on the protection of individuals with regard to processing of personal data and on the free movement of such data.

At any time you can request access to the personal data which we hold about you. We reserve our right to make a small administrative charge to deal with such requests. You can also request that we correct any such data which is wrong. All data access and correction requests should be made in writing and addressed to: Educational Technologies Limited Customer Service, 10/F, Block C, Seaview Estate, 2-8 Watson Road, North Point, Hong Kong.
