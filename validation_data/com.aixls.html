Just created AIOS, the whole stack of Team has been thinking about how to respect and secure the privacy of users, as a "user-centric" service, will inevitably come into contact with a lot of your personal information. How to ensure your trust in us? To know that the user's trust is the fundamental to survive the AIOS; lost your trust, which means that AIOS will completely fail. The result of our concern is that we believe that the only way to win your trust is a high degree of self-discipline. As a mobile Internet products, AIOS must be cautious about the privacy of users, to avoid repeat the mistakes of the industry predecessors. So, as early as March 2014, even before we wanted to use the name "AIOS", we made reference to the international Internet business standards, developed the principle of user privacy, as our related work guidance. Over the past year, the whole stack of products from a single idea, gradually expanded into a "platform" of the system services, but we wrote a year ago, "AIOS" user privacy three principles, but has not changed:

In the following, I will explain in detail how "AIOS" actually practices these principles. When you use, we receive the information in the use of "AIOS" process, and most of the mobile products, we will collect some use of information and sent to our server. The information received by our servers can be divided into two categories, one on how users use "AIOS" information, and the other is the user-owned data summary statistics. Let's take a closer look at what information "AIOS" will send. For example, the application service upgrade, what cell phone do you use? Is Android version 4.0 or 4.3? Are you using Apple 5 or the latest 6s? How big is your screen and memory? How is your phone hardware configuration? This information also includes a unique identification number for your computer and mobile phone. This information helps to help us arrange the order of the work, for example, through this information, we can make improvements to make you better experience. AIOS itself information. For example, how often do you start AIOS? When will you stop using AIOS? We use this information to determine the effect of our various promotional methods to help us understand how to make more users know AIOS. It is through this information, we found that more than 85% of the AIOS users through the word of mouth to know our, here to the way and you say: Thank you! Information about application quality. For example, is there a crash in the process of using "AIOS"? If so, what is the cause of the crash? How does "AIOS" run on your phone? We use this information to improve the quality of the application to ensure that "AIOS" can be quickly and stably running on more users' handsets. For example, by constantly analyzing this information, we have reduced the collapse rate of products from nearly 10% to only a few thousandths in the past year. Information about the phone connection. For example, can you successfully use the "AIOS" connection on the phone, if not, what happens wrong? Is there any possibility of interference? The usage statistics of each function. For example, what are the most frequently and most frequently used features in AIOS? How many apps, music, videos, and ebooks have you viewed via AIOS?
